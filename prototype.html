Maven is a question and answer service that revolves around "life questions". At its core, its about connecting curious users with those who have relevant experience to spark engaging face-to-face conversations.

To fully experience the app here is what you need to know:

  • When requested for profile input, please type them into a word document since you can't type on the prototype
  • Just skip the video recording step right now since this prototype doesn't have that functionality

Steps:

  1. Ask a question
    1. please write down what question you "asked", we just want to know what you thought of putting in.
    2. here are the videos for the profiles:
      1. Sarah
      2. Talu
      3. Sam
    3. There will be some profiles you won't be able to select yes to and some that you won't be able to select no to. This is intentional.
    4. When you get to the third profile, press the home button and go to the next step.
  2. Answer questions
    1. here are the videos for the profiles:
      1. Tori
      2. Vince
      3. Vicky
      4. When you get to the third profile, press the home button and go to the next step.
  3. Go to answer questions page, click on Talu, and then click on the setting bar
    1. go through the pages until you get back to the home Q/A page. Then you are done.
